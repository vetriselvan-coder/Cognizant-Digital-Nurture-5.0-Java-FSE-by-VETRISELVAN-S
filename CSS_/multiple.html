Community Bulletin

The city council has announced a series of new development projects aimed at improving infrastructure and public services across all wards. These projects include road expansion, improved drainage systems, and better street lighting to enhance safety for residents.

In addition to infrastructure upgrades, the council is also focusing on community welfare programs. Free health check-up camps will be organized in different neighborhoods, and educational workshops will be conducted for students and job seekers.

Residents are encouraged to participate in upcoming town hall meetings to share their suggestions and concerns. The council believes that active public involvement is essential for building a better and more sustainable city.
